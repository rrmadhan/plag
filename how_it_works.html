Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our plagiarism detection process

How accurate is your plagiarism detection?

Our AI-powered system achieves over 99% accuracy in detecting plagiarized content. It uses advanced algorithms that can identify even heavily paraphrased text and cross-references against the largest database of online and academic content.

What file formats do you support?

We support all major document formats including DOC, DOCX, PDF, TXT, RTF, and ODT. You can also directly paste text into our editor. For large documents or batch processing, we offer additional options through our premium plans.

How long does the scanning process take?

For most standard documents (under 10,000 words), the scanning process typically takes less than 60 seconds. Larger documents may take slightly longer, but our optimized system ensures you get results faster than traditional plagiarism checkers.

Is my content secure and private?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and never store your content after generating your report. Your documents and results are completely confidential and are never shared with third parties or added to our database.
