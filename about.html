Our Story

We are a team of final-year engineering students from Loyola-ICAM College of Engineering and Technology (LICET), Chennai, united by the ambition to solve real problems using modern technology. During our academic journey, we observed a growing need for trustworthy and intelligent plagiarism detection — not just for universities, but for writers, researchers, and content creators across industries.

Most existing plagiarism tools rely heavily on outdated methods: basic text matching, slow processing, poor accuracy, and limited understanding of evolving writing patterns. This gap inspired us to act.What began as a standard academic project soon transformed into a mission: to build an AI-powered plagiarism solution that is advanced, user-centric, and future-ready.

This is our journey — from a simple idea to meaningful innovation. We believe every idea deserves to be valued and respected. We stand for honesty, creativity, and trusted content. This is our contribution to a world with authentic voices. This is our journey.
